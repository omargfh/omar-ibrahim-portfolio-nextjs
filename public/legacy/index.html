Welcome!

I am Omar Ibrahim, or you can just call me Adam, and I am a poet, computer science enthusiast/developer, and a graphic artists. I am also a debater/public speaker and have enjoyed a while exploring entrepreneurship and international politics. I aspire to work in the field of education where I can implement computer science in the expansion of educational opportunities, especially in literature. Currently, I study a STEM-focused Honors curriculum at STEM High School for Boys - 6th of October.

I aspire to pursue higher education in the United States, somewhere that allows me to reach out to unexplored domains of knowledge and supports my desires to learn and discover new fields.
