Art is Life!

What happens when you leave a pencil, a sheet of paper, a copy of Adobe Photoshop, and an enthusiastic 10-year-old in a room? If you would take a word, then I can tell you that a lot happens. If you will not take my word, however, why do not I show you?

It was seven years ago when I began fiddling around with my first copy of Adobe Photoshop. My uncle was an arts professional and watching him create fabuolous designs in every family gathering pushed me to aspire to learn as well. It took time to get acquianted with the dozens of tools and menus and even more time to get some creativity shining.

Today, I work with Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD, Adobe InDesign, and more! Through those seven years, I have been a president of social media (one other secret hobby) and media of Disney Bel-Masry, Dubsphere, Advancit, TEDxYouth@MaadiSTEM, among others. Additionally, I was CSO of Mirus (graphic design startup), have filled-in as a designer whenever needed in any ECA I joined, and made dozens of projects for myself or lesiure.

Enjoy a selection of my favorite works here.
