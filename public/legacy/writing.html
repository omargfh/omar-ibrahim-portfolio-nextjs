Poetry

A collection of poems in different genres from epistle to spoken words and varying in length between short (<100 words) and long (>500 words). Each piece represents an emotional, philosophical or social topic in the life of the author.

Prose and Fiction

Long narrative essays written to portray ideas, fantasies, creative thoughts, and anecdotes of the author's life. Language shifts from casual to tense in some pieces.

Scriptwriting

A collection of scripts written for personal use or done for/on behalf of others/other communities. My journey with scripwriting began with TED-Ed Club, so both my TED talks scripts are included as well.

Academic Writing

Essays, reports, and porfolios done for school or as part of extracurricular activies. Most of the work listed here is collaborative. Authors listed in each article.
